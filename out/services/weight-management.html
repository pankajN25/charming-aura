Common Questions

How fast will I lose weight?▼

Most clients lose 1-2 kg per week in the first month, then 0.5-1 kg weekly. Average weight loss is 12-18kg in 4 months.

Will I feel hungry on your meal plans?▼

No. Our plans are designed to keep you satisfied while in a calorie deficit. We focus on whole foods and adequate protein.

Do I need to exercise?▼

Weight loss is 80% nutrition, 20% exercise. We recommend light activities but focus on diet for results.

What if I have dietary restrictions?▼

We create plans for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary needs. Customization is our specialty.

Is there a guarantee?▼

Yes! 30-day money-back guarantee if you follow the plan and don't see results. Plus lifetime support access.
